The Best Movies According to Christian

My top 3 movies of all time

Rush Hour Trilogy

By far my favourite movie series as I love the comedy writing/acting and the two actors have very good chemsitry on and off screen. Always puts me in a good mood while watching it and it demonstrates the importance of family and friendship

Star Wars Series

I love the story writing in this movie especially from movies 1-6. It put's you through an emotional rollercoaster throughout and the action scenes are to die for!

Your Name Animated Movie

I love anime hence I had to mention one of the best if not then the best anime movie which is Your Name. The story is truly compelling and I love the soundtrack. The way they portray the characters in the film is just perfect as it tells a story like no other. But the most impressive thing about the film is defintely the animation.
